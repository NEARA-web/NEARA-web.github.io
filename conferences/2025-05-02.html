Spring 2025 Conference

May 2 to 4 near Fishkill, NY

The Spring 2025 NEARA Conference will be held at Sharpe Reservation's Camp Mariah, near Fishkill New York on the first weekend of May.

Program

We're working on it.... If you would like to present, contact us at Spring 2025 Program.

We will be holding our annual members meeting at this conference.

NOTE: The only way to attend the conference will be to pre-register. Your name must be on the list of attendees in order to be admitted onto the Sharpe Reservation.
