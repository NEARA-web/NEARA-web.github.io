Events of Interest

These events include both online webinars as well as in-person events, sponsored by NEARA or by other organizations.

Have an event you want to add to this calendar? Send email to calendar@neara.org.

Subscribing to this public calendar in your own online calendar

For Google Calendar, go to your Google Calendar website (not in the Calendar app).

  1. On the left, next to "Other calendars", click the "+" button (Add other calendars).
  2. Click on "Subscribe to calendar".
  3. In the "Add calendar" box, enter: calendar@neara.org
  4. The NEARA Calendar will show up in your list of calendars.
  5. Use the checkbox to show and hide all events from the NEARA Calendar.

For the Apple Calendar app.

  1. Copy this ICS link:
    https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/calendar%40neara.org/public/basic.ics
  2. Go to Settings > Accounts & Passwords > Add Account > Other
  3. Tap "Add Subscribed Calendar".
  4. In the "Server" section, paste that ICS link (from step 1).
  5. Enter a "Description" such as "NEARA Calendar".
  6. Tap the "Use SSL" switch.
  7. Tap the "Save" button.
